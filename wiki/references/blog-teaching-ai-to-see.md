## Motivation
Published narrative documenting the research journey. Useful as a high-level overview of the project's most significant work.

## Summary

The blog post traces the path from attempting mechanical shape extraction through increasingly sophisticated 2D approaches, all of which failed, to the breakthrough of using 3D reconstruction as the foundation for 2D illustration.

Key narrative beats:
1. Initial attempts at edge detection (scoring 0.002/10)
2. Progressive 2D refinements that all hit the same ceiling
3. The insight that shadows are the fundamental problem
4. The pivot to 3D reconstruction
5. Building the self-correcting feedback loop
6. Correction learning as a potentially novel contribution
