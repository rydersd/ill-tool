# Storyboarding System

> Brief: 30 tools for multi-panel narrative production — panels, camera, staging, transitions, beat sheets, audio sync, EDL/OTIO export.
> Tags: storyboard, panels, camera, staging, narrative, production
> Created: 2026-04-03
> Updated: 2026-04-03

## Motivation
Enable full storyboard production in Illustrator — from script breakdown through exportable edit decision lists.

## Overview

30 tools covering the complete storyboarding pipeline from script analysis to final export.

## Capabilities

- **Panel Management** — Templates with configurable grids, panel composition, thumbnail grids, promotion from thumbnails to full panels
- **Script Integration** — Script-to-storyboard conversion, beat sheet generation, shot list generation
- **Camera & Staging** — Camera notation (pan/zoom/truck/dolly), lighting notation, staging system for character placement
- **Scene Organization** — Scene manager, scene graph, scene composition, sequence assembly
- **Transitions** — Transition planning with validation between panels
- **Production Metadata** — Director markup, production notes, dialogue layout, continuity checking (basic and enhanced)
- **Multi-Character** — Multi-character placement and prop management
- **Audio** — Audio sync (basic and enhanced), beat timing
- **Export** — EDL (edit decision list), OTIO (OpenTimelineIO), batch export (PNG, PDF, JSON, AE JSX)
